About
Engaging with themes of materiality and transformation, Erik creates sculptures and installations that reveal the dynamism of objects and contradict anthropocentrism. Botany, geology and mineralogy exhilarate his investigation of structure and fabrication techniques in creating a gestalt. His works, composed of wood, glass, stone, glass, and luminous inert gasses emphasize the intrinsic characteristics of the materials and confront the viewer with an otherworldly affect. His ontological investigation of materials and forms aims to connect the physical world to the intangible, scientific discovery to mysticism, and the present day to an ancient past.
